Sookan

Sookan is the narrator and main character of Year of Impossible Goodbyes. Sookan is a very shy 10 year old Korean girl. She does not know much about the war and outside world since her family tries so hard to protect her from it. She is brave, kind, imaginative and most of all peaceful.

Grandfather

Grandfather is one of the family's favorite members. He is wise, kind, and thoughtful. Since he is the oldest man in the family evryone must respect him. He loves meditating under his tree and cannot wait for the Japanese invasion to end.
