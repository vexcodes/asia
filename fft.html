Xiao Hong and The Giant Rice Plant

Once upon a time, there was a poor chinese farmer who had one son. They were very hungry because they had no money for food. one day xiao hong(the son) finds a man on the street selling rice seeds for a very low price of only 1 yuan. Xiao Hong needs this rice very much so he uses all his money he got from spring festival to buy the seeds. Xiao Hong arrives home and plants the seeds, a few weeks later the seeds start sprouting into a plant. Xiao Hong and Mother were very exited that they had a renewable food source when suddenly the plant grew 300 meters tall. Xiao Hong being the adventures young boy he is wants to climb the plant to harvest the rice on top, mother thinks this to be too dangerous but Xiao Hong climbs it anyway. It took him one day but he fianly reached the top and when he got there he did not see any rice but instead a giant palace. Xiao Hong was getting ready to go back down and tell mother they had a new home when suddenly, THUMP THUMP, a giant creature known only as a oni appeard, Xiao Hong was scared out of his mind. The oni scooped him up and shouted out loud I WILL BOIL YOU IN A STEW, Xiao Hong was hopeless as the oni carried him into the palace, ching chong bing bong i will eat you in a human dumpling he said. They Finally reached the kitchen, everything was so big Xiao Hong relised when he saw it, a pairing knife the size of a human sword Xiao Hong knew this to be his way out. A few minuets later the oni set him in a jar but forgot to put on the lid, Xiao Hong quickly scurried out of the jar and got the knife. He walked up to the oni and started madly slashing at him, the oni not expecting this fell to the floor and cracked his skull and died letting out a long bellowing roar. Xiao Hong did not want to stay around and find out what other horrors were in store here and ended up taking a few giant dumpling. A few days later Xiao Hong reached the bottem of the rice plant, to see many military officals standing there, puzzeld Xiao Hong walked up to them where his mother explaind, The monster you killed up there let out such a loud screem the officals came all the way from the capital to investigate, turns out you slew the monster which has caused the death of the emporer, if you accept you are the new leader of china for doing such a brave act. Tears welled up in Xiao Hongs eyes as he accepted, for so many years he and his mother were living in poverty but finally they could live a succesful life. xiao hong ended up being the yougest emporer in history and founded modern day china. They all lived happily every after.
